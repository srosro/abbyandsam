Directions

Weekend activities will be centered in Mendocino, CA.

Flying?

The closest airport is Santa Rosa, a two hour drive from Mendocino. However, flights to San Francisco International Airport (a 3.5 hour drive) are often less expensive.

Traffic! If you are choosing to fly into SFO, the traffic from the airport to San Francisco, over the Golden Gate bridge and into Marin County will be especially bad on Thursday and Friday afternoon. We suggest landing before 1PM or after 6PM.

 

Driving?

From the South (including the airport): 101 North to 128 West to Mendocino.

From the North: I-5 South to US-199 South to US-101 South to CA-1 South to Mendocino.
